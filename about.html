Mohammed Jouhar

Data Professional & Analytics Specialist

I'm Mohammed Jouhar, a data professional with over 10 years of experience in analytics, automation, and data strategy. I've worked in retail, data migration, AI agents, and more. I created this blog to share practical, easy-to-understand insights in data, AI, and career growth for aspiring analysts, professionals, and curious learners.

Professional Experience

Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to work across various industries and tackle diverse data challenges. My experience spans:

  • Retail Analytics: Developing insights from customer behavior and sales patterns
  • Data Migration: Managing complex data transformation and system transitions
  • AI Agents: Implementing and optimizing automated solutions for business processes
  • Data Strategy: Creating roadmaps for organizations to leverage their data assets
  • Analytics Automation: Building systems that deliver insights at scale

My Approach to Data

I believe that data is most valuable when it's accessible and actionable. My approach focuses on:

  • Translating complex technical concepts into practical, understandable insights
  • Bridging the gap between data specialists and business stakeholders
  • Emphasizing ethical data practices and responsible AI development
  • Continuous learning and adaptation to emerging technologies

About This Blog

My dataworks is a platform where I share knowledge, experiences, and insights from my journey in the data world. Here, you'll find:

  • Practical tutorials and guides for data analytics tools and techniques
  • Insights into AI agents and automation technologies
  • Career advice for aspiring and established data professionals
  • Case studies and project breakdowns from real-world scenarios
  • Learning notes and reflections on emerging trends in data and AI

My goal is to create content that is both accessible to beginners and valuable to experienced professionals, focusing on practical applications rather than just theory.
